Polyurethane (PU) is a polymer — a molecular "network" forming a resistant, flexible and durable plastic.

**Petrochemical PU** = petroleum-derived. Emits toxic solvents, high carbon footprint.
**Vegetable PU (VPU)** = derived from **castor oil**. 100% solids, solvent-free, biodegradable under suitable conditions.

The difference is the raw material: castor is a plant grown in the Brazilian semi-arid region, renewable, and does not compete with food crops.
